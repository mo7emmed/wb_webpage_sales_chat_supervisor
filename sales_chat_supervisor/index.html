Sales Chat Supervisor

About the Role

We are seeking a highly motivated and detail-oriented Sales Chat Supervisor to lead, supervise, and optimize our customer communication channels across Meta Business Suite (Messenger + Instagram DM) and WhatsApp. This role is crucial to our sales success, as our business depends heavily on the quality, speed, and effectiveness of our replies.

The ideal candidate will bring a strong sense of customer empathy, sales psychology, and leadership to transform our chat-based customer service into a high-performing, revenue-generating resource.

Position Details

  • Location: Baghdad – Al Mansour
  • Working Hours: 12:00 PM – 09:00 PM (Saturday to Thursday)
  • Salary: 1,200,000 IQD per month, with potential performance-based bonuses

Key Responsibilities

  1. Team Supervision & Coaching
    • Monitor message replies daily to ensure proper tone, clarity, and customer satisfaction.
    • Provide insights about dealing with customer psychology to ensure empathetic, persuasive, and trust-building communication.
    • Provide regular feedback, coaching, and guidance to team members.
    • Train new or underperforming agents and help standardize tone across the team.
  2. Messaging Optimization
    • Develop and manage a system of smart reply templates, personalized scripts, FAQs, and voice + text message combinations.
    • Ensure every customer interaction is warm, helpful, and designed to drive conversion.
  3. Performance Tracking
    • Track and report on key performance indicators such as:
      • Response time
      • Message quality
      • Sales from chat
      • Missed opportunities
    • Run weekly or bi-weekly quality audits and performance reviews.
  4. Sales Support & Process Improvement
    • Propose and test improvements in messaging flow (e.g., welcome messages, quick reply buttons, link structure).
    • Work closely with marketing/sales to align on campaigns, promotions, and messaging tone.

Requirements

  • Proven experience in customer service, chat support, or social media messaging roles.
  • Ability to lead the digital sales team without micromanaging — firm but friendly.
  • Strong knowledge of Meta Business Suite (Facebook/Instagram inbox tools).
  • Native-level Arabic communication – Iraqi accent, with warm and clear writing tone.
  • Strong command of English is a plus.
  • Excellent communication and coaching skills.
  • Sales mindset: ability to convert conversations into actions or orders.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience working in eCommerce, retail, or hospitality messaging.
  • Experience with reporting or dashboard tools (even in Excel/Google Sheets).

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary with potential bonuses.
  • Opportunity to directly impact sales and growth.
  • Friendly, collaborative environment.
  • The freedom to build systems, coach a team, and make things better.
